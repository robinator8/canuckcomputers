ABOUT CANUCK COMPUTERS

We design, build, and setup quality custom PCs for affordable prices. Our three pricing levels allow you to get exactly what you need in a PC. With our PREMIUM and DELUXE packages, we individually pick the best parts according to your price range and use case. Canuck PCs are carfully hand built with premium components to ensure a long lifespan in our PCs. We are based out of Wellsboro PA, and service the surrounding area. If you live further away, contact us and we'll see what we can do!

No Bloatware

Most manufactioners load new computers with pre-installed software called "bloatware." We keep our PCs bloatware free so that they stay running faster for longer.

Premium Parts

The better the parts the longer things last. For this reason, we only choose premium parts from reputable manufactioners.

Quick Turnover

Unlike large corporations that may take weeks to build and ship PCs, we work around the clock to provide you with the fastest service possible.

In Home Setup

With our DELUXE package, you don't even have to worry about computer setup. We'll bring you your PC and set it up in your house!
